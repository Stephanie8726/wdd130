Overview

Purpose

The purpose of a rafting company is to build revenue by providing thrilling outdoor experiences to adventure seekers, families with children, and corporate groups while promoting safety and teamwork.

Audience

Q: Who are the target customers?

A: Our target audience for the water rafting website are likely to be active individuals in their 20s to 40s who enjoy outdoor activities and are seeking an exciting and memorable experience.

Q: What do they want?

A: A great adventure and a thrilling challenge in a scenic, outdoor setting.

Q: What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

A: In-depth details on the various types of rafting trips available, including pricing, safety protocols, required equipment, minimum requirements, location, availability, customer reviews, as well as other activities and services in the area.
