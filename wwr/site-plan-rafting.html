Overview

Purpose

The purpose of a water rafting company is to build revenue by providing thrilling outdoor experiences to adventure seekers, families with children, and corporate groups while promoting safety and teamwork.

Audience

Q: Who are our ideal audience?

A: Our target audience for the water rafting website are adventurous individuals who enjoy outdoor activities and are seeking an exciting and memorable experience.

Q: What type of families would enjoy rafting with us?

A: Families with children ages 8 and up who are seeking an exciting and safe adventure together.

Q: What are some of the benefits of rafting for corporate groups?

A: Rafting is an excellent team-building activity that promotes communication, problem-solving, and trust among team members.
