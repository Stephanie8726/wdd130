Philippines

Philippines is an archipelagic country located in Southeast Asia, known for its stunning natural beauty and diverse culture. With over 7,000 islands, it boasts some of the world's best beaches, coral reefs, and natural wonders like the Chocolate Hills and Cordilleras Rice Terraces. Philippines is the world's largest exporter of coconuts and tropical fruits such as bananas and pineapples. It is also one of the world's top producers of seafood, with tuna, milkfish, and shrimp being some of the most popular exports.
